About

Hi! I'm Ingrid Arreola. Data Analyst. My interests are data-driven storytelling for social change.

Data Visualization Enthusiast. Data Analyst who Loves to learn, share her work, and increase data literacy.

I’m excited about the intersection of technology for development and social good.

I have experience in nonprofits, where I was on the data team at a small nonprofit focusing on providing data and research for Hoosier youth well-being. The project 2019 Indiana KIDS COUNT® Data Book reflects this work.

My next role in the nonprofit space was at IU Health, where I was on an enterprise wide data team. The Business and Clinical Intelligence team focused on creating enterprise dashboards and I focused on the community health team’s iniatives. I focused on building dashboards about Tobacco Treatment, Obesity, and supporting other projects like Infant and Maternal Health. During this time I learned how to use tools such as Power BI, SQL Server Management Studio, Visual Studio, Azure Data Studio, and Azure DevOps. I also worked with data in the cloud – Azure – and navigated through upwards of 50+ million rows to create the tables needed for the various dashboards I was building.

My current role is at Salesforce, where I am on the Finance & Strategy team. Prior to my roles in data, I was a health volunteer for Peace Corps Paraguay where I thoroughly enjoyed grassroots community health projects.
