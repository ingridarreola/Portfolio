About Me

Life long learner who loves projects involving data visualization. A data analyst who loves to share work, and increase data literacy.

My background is in data visualization and data analysis. I believe that data should inform decisions and I strive to create data products and dashboards to be a go-to source for the latest research and information. I’m excited about the intersection of technology for development and social good. I have experience in nonprofit focusing on providing data and research for Hoosier youth well-being. Prior to my data background I was a health volunteer for Peace Corps Paraguay where I thoroughly enjoyed grassroots community health projects.
